About My Technical Journey

I am a Senior Software Architect with over 19 years of expertise in building mission-critical systems. My journey is driven by Epistemology—a deep desire to understand the logic and first principles behind every line of code.

Research Philosophy: "To secure the future of AI, we must master its past." From auditing JDK source code to deriving neural gradients, I believe technical depth is the only path to architectural stability.

🔬 Deep Learning R&D: The IRNN Foundation

During my Master's research, I focused on the "Vanishing Gradient" problem, a fundamental hurdle in recurrent architectures. Instead of relying on high-level frameworks, I implemented Identity Recurrent Neural Networks (IRNN) from scratch in Java.

  • Mathematical Derivation: Hand-coded BPTT (Backpropagation Through Time) logic.
  • Stabilization: Leveraged identity matrix initialization to preserve signal flow across ultra-long sequences.
  • Legacy: This research laid the groundwork for my understanding of "Identity Drift" in complex data systems.

🛡️ Modern Challenge: Responsible AI & SQL-Guard

Today, I apply that same "First-Principles" rigor to Generative AI. My latest project, LLM-SQL-Guard, is a direct evolution of my work in sequence logic. It serves as a security layer to ensure that AI-generated queries are safe, accurate, and aligned with enterprise schemas.
